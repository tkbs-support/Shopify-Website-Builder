## What We Fix Per Collection

1. **SEO Title** — Keyword-rich, 50-65 chars, removes backslash hierarchy
2. **Meta Description** — 140-155 chars with product details, location, and CTA
3. **Collection Description** — 2-3 sentences with product attributes (styles, sizes, materials, brands carried)

## Scope

- **Tier 1:** 7 pages — highest priority, do first
- **Tier 2:** 16 pages — do after Tier 1
- **Tier 3:** 12 pages — do after Tier 2
- **Total:** 35 pages
- **Remaining 506 collections** are deep subcategories — lower priority, could be scripted later

## Non-Navigable Collections (do NOT optimize)

~288 collections are not linked from the site navigation. These include:
- **Sale events:** "1/24/26 60% OFF Sale", "38th Anniversary Sale", "Customer Appreciation", "Pink Friday", etc.
- **Seasonal promos:** "A Golden New Year", "Cyber Monday", "Give Back Friday"
- **Duplicate/orphaned:** "Accessories \ Accessories \ Belts" (two versions), various unused subcategories
- **Internal/system:** "Discount Exclusions", "Dollar or Less Products", "maxretail"

These should NOT be optimized for SEO — they're temporary, event-specific, or not meant to be discovered via search. They could potentially be cleaned up or hidden from search engines with `noindex` tags as a future project.

## Important Note

Collection titles, descriptions, and meta fields are **store-wide** (not theme-specific).
Changes go live immediately when pushed — they are not tied to the duplicate theme.
Owner approval is required before proceeding.
